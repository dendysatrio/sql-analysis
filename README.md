# 🗄️ Revo Grocers SQL Sales Performance Analysis

SQL-based analysis of a grocery retail dataset to uncover product performance,
customer behavior, and revenue drivers across multiple categories.

**Program:** RevoU Full Stack Data Analytics — SQL Module  
**Period:** Nov 2025

---

## 🎯 Business Context

Revo Grocers aims to optimize sales strategies, enhance customer experience,
and maximize revenue through data-driven insights. This analysis answers
8 key business questions using SQL queries on a multi-table relational dataset.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

**Category Performance:**
- **Confections** is the #1 revenue category — over Rp556M after discount,
  highest unique customers, and 99.85% repeat purchase rate
- **Meat, Poultry, and Cereals** follow closely — together with Confections
  they contribute over 44% of total revenue

**Revenue Drivers:**
- Revenue is **volume-driven, not price-driven** — mid-priced categories
  outperform premium ones
- High-priced categories (Grain, Dairy, Snails) do NOT generate
  proportionally higher revenue

**Customer Behavior:**
- Customer counts are consistently high (~97K) across all categories
- Repeat purchase rates are extremely strong (97–99%) across the board
- Top customer (ID 94800) generated 130K+ in cumulative spend with
  steady, consistent buying behavior
